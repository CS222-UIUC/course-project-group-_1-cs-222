Our motivating problem follows the rise in urban infrastructure deterioration in the past decade,specifically with roads. We wanted to find a way to make it easier to solve road conditionproblems. Therefore, we strive to build a mobile application to detect, document, and reportroad conditions on-the-fly. Our aim is to seamlessly integrate a valuable tool into acommonly-used piece of technology.









This project is still in progress.

Powered by TensorFlow, Firebase, Python, Google Maps API, JS, CSS, HTML5
